Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

CookVision ("we", "our", or "the App") is a mobile application developed by 4orce-tech. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use CookVision.

1. Information We Collect

CookVision collects the following types of information:

  • Photos and Images: When you use the camera or gallery feature to scan your fridge contents, the images are processed to identify food products. Images are sent to Google Gemini API for analysis and are not stored on our servers.
  • Subscription Data: If you subscribe to CookVision Pro, purchase information is processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store and managed by RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment details (credit card numbers, etc.).
  • App Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage analytics through Firebase Analytics to improve the app experience. This includes crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Local Storage: Recent scans, favorite recipes, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze photos of your fridge and identify food products
  • To generate personalized recipe suggestions based on identified ingredients
  • To manage your subscription status
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

3. Third-Party Services

CookVision uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: For image analysis and recipe generation. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase (Google): For analytics and crash reporting. Subject to Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Pexels API: For recipe images. Subject to Pexels Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Photos taken within the app are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on external servers.
  • Scan history and favorites are stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Camera and Photo Library Access

CookVision requests access to your device camera and photo library solely for the purpose of scanning food items in your fridge. This access is used only when you actively choose to take a photo or select an image from your gallery.

6. Children's Privacy

CookVision is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your local app data by uninstalling the app
  • Manage or cancel your subscription through your device's app store settings
  • Request information about data we process about you

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
